About the Symposium

Building a stronger health data community in Africa.

Our Mission

The REDCap Africa Symposium brings together a vibrant community of researchers, data managers, and technologists. Our goal is to foster collaboration, share expertise, and improve data quality for health research across the continent.

Since our inception, we have focused on practical training, peer-to-peer learning, and establishing robust governance frameworks for data sovereignty.

Who Should Attend?

  • Researchers looking to optimize their data collection.
  • Data Managers seeking best practices in QC and validation.
  • IT Professionals managing REDCap infrastructure.
  • Clinicians interested in electronic data capture.
