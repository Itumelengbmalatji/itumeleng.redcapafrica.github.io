About the Symposium

Building Capacity, Driving Collaboration

Our Mission

The REDCap Africa Symposium brings together a vibrant community of researchers, data managers, and technologists. Our goal is to foster collaboration, share expertise, and improve data quality for health research across the continent.

Since our inception, we have focused on hands-on training, peer-to-peer learning, and establishing collaborations across institutions.

What We Do?

  • Capacity Building Empowering institutions with skills to manage health data effectively.
  • Innovation Showcasing cutting-edge REDCap applications and integrations.
  • Collaboration Creating networks that strengthen research across Africa.
  • Governance Promoting ethical and secure data practices
