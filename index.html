Why Attend?

Join a vibrant community of researchers, data managers, and IT professionals from across Africa and beyond. The REDCap Africa Symposium 2026 is your opportunity to:

  • Share best practices in data collection and management.
  • Discuss data governance and ethical considerations.
  • Explore the future of REDCap and digital health solutions in Africa.
  • Network with peers, innovators, and thought leaders.
