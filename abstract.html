Abstract Submission

Share your work at the REDCap Africa Symposium 2026.

Submit Your Abstract

We invite you to submit an abstract showcasing how you have used REDCap in operational, clinical, or research settings. Submissions should highlight practical applications, innovative approaches, and lessons learned from real-world implementations. Whether your work focuses on data collection, workflow optimization, system integration, or supporting research outcomes, we encourage you to share your experience and insights with the broader REDCap community.
Abstracts should clearly describe:

  • The context and objectives of your project or use case
  • How REDCap was implemented or utilized
  • Key features, tools, or customizations employed
  • The outcomes, impact, or benefits achieved
  • Any challenges encountered and lessons learned

We welcome submissions from a wide range of sectors, including academic research, healthcare, public health programs, non-profit organizations, and operational data management environments.
