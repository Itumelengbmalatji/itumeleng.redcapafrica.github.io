Accommodation

Attendees requiring accommodation at the Lagoon Beach Hotel & Spa at the conference block booking rate should email the Events Department at events@lagoonbeachhotel.co.za to have their booking linked to the symposium block rate at the hotel.

Travel Information

✈️ Flights

Delegates can fly into Cape Town International Airport (CPT), which offers regular domestic and international routes. Most major airlines serve the city, with convenient connections from Johannesburg, Durban and key African hubs.

🚕 Transport

Ride‑hailing services such as Uber and Bolt are widely available across the city. Metered taxis and shuttle services also operate from the airport and major hotels making travel to and from the venue straightforward.
